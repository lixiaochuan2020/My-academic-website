## About Me

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Prof. [Chenyan Xiong](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~cx/). My research interests lie in Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing, with a particular focus on data-centric large language models. I am keen on:

- The relationship between data and model performance.
- Tracing the origins of model capabilities (e.g., reasoning) to their data sources.
- Generating higher-quality and more effective training data.

Prior to attending CMU, I earned my bachelor's degree in Software Engineering with the highest honors from Tsinghua University in 2025. During my exchange at the University of Hong Kong, I was fortunate to work with Prof. [Tao Yu](https://taoyds.github.io/) on computer-use agents.
